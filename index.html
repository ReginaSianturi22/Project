About us

Manokwari

Discover the natural charm of Manokwari with white sandy beaches, tropical rainforests and stunning waterfalls. Explore Dorey Bay and experience its underwater riches.

Sorong

Sorong is a port city that has a rich history. Visit historical sites and enjoy local delicacies at traditional markets.

Timika

Timika is the gateway to Raja Ampat and Biak Island. Enjoy the unrivaled beauty of the underwater world by snorkeling and diving.

Nabire

Nabire is a city located in Papua Province, Indonesia, and is famous for its natural beauty and spectacular underwater life.
